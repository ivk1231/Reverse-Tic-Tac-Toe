How to Play

Reverse Tic-Tac-Toe is the opposite of regular Tic-Tac-Toe:

  • Take turns placing your symbol (X or O) on the grid
  • Force your opponent to get three of their symbols in a row
  • If you get three of your symbols in a row, you lose!
  • Lines can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
